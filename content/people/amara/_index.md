## Thesis Title

Exploring alternative problem-solving approaches for the factory of the future using virtual and augmented reality

## Abstract

My PhD thesis title is "Exploring alternative problem-solving approaches using virtual and augmented reality for the factory of the future", its subject consists of using Augmented Reality (AR) by an operator and Virtual Reality (VR) by an expert to collaborate remotely on a problem solving task. The expert explores different approaches to solve a problem faced by an operator in the industrial field using specific visual cues, real-time synchronized digital twins and real-time exchange between the operator and the expert to explore the problem and propose a solution that takes into account the real conditions of the operator and the feasibility of the proposed solution.
