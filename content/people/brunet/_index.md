## Thesis Title

Activités collectives dans les télé-cliniques immersives pour la prévention en santé mentale

## Abstract

My thesis is about collective activities in immersive meta-clinics for mental health prevention. The goal is to study the tools and process needed to improve the experience of (therapeutic) group discussions and group activities in VR
